1. Poles: What happens when you bring the North pole of one magnet near the North pole of another?

Answer
They repel (like poles repel).
Easy

2. Magnetic Field: Where is the magnetic field strongest on a bar magnet?

Answer
At the poles (North and South ends).

🟡 Medium

Medium

3. Electromagnets: How can you increase the strength of an electromagnet made with a battery, wire, and a nail?

Answer
1. Increase the current (bigger battery).
2. Increase the number of loops of wire around the nail.
Medium

4. Faraday's Law: How can you induce an electric current in a loop of wire using a magnet?

Answer
By moving the magnet through the loop or moving the loop past the magnet (changing the magnetic field).

🔴 Hard

Hard

5. Generators: Describe the energy transformation that occurs in an electric generator.

Answer
A generator converts Mechanical Energy (spinning motion) into Electrical Energy using electromagnetic induction.
