1. Charge Attraction: If you have two objects, one positive and one negative, do they attract or repel?

Answer
They attract (opposite charges attract).

2. Conductors: Name a common material that is a good electrical conductor.

Answer
Any metal, such as Copper, Aluminum, or Gold.

3. Coulomb's Law: If the distance between two charges is tripled (3x), what happens to the electrical force between them?

Answer
Like gravity, it follows the inverse square law. (1/3)² = 1/9th of the original force.
Medium

4. Induction: How can you charge an object without actually touching it with another charged object?

Answer
Through Induction: Bring a charged object near a conductor, then ground the conductor to allow charges to move off or onto it.

5. Van de Graaff: Why does your hair stand up when you touch a Van de Graaff generator?

Answer
The generator transfers a massive amount of same-type charge to your body. Each strand of hair becomes charged with the same polarity and repels every other strand, causing them to push away from each other and stand up.
