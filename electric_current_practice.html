🔌 Electric Current & Circuits

🟢 Easy

Easy

1. Ohm's Law: If a circuit has a voltage of 12 V and a resistance of 4 Ohms, what is the current?

Answer
I = V / R = 12 / 4 = 3 Amperes.
Easy

2. Units: What are the units for Voltage, Current, and Resistance?

Answer
Voltage: Volts (V), Current: Amperes (A), Resistance: Ohms (Ω).

🟡 Medium

Medium

3. Power: A toaster draws 10 Amps at 120 Volts. How much power does it use?

Answer
P = IV = 10 * 120 = 1200 Watts.
Medium

4. Series Circuits: If you add more lightbulbs to a series circuit, what happens to the total brightness of the bulbs?

Answer
The brightness decreases because total resistance increases, which reduces the total current in the circuit.

🔴 Hard

Hard

5. Parallel Circuits: Why are houses wired in parallel rather than series?

Answer
1. So appliances can be turned on/off independently (in series, if one goes out, all go out).
2. So every appliance gets the full voltage (120V in the US).
